What is Liberstad?

Founded in 2017, Liberstad is a private city project in Norway built on the principles of:

  • Individual Freedom
  • Private Property Rights
  • Free Market Solutions
  • Voluntary Cooperation

Understanding Voluntaryism

Voluntaryism is the philosophy that all human interactions should be:

  • Voluntary
  • Free from Coercion
  • Based on Mutual Consent
  • Respecting Individual Rights
